Chiron Privacy Policy

What we collect, how we use it, and how to have your identity removed.

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Chiron is a study and exam-practice product operated by Thrive Tech. This policy covers the Chiron web application. Thrive Tech is the controller of the data described here.

What we collect

  • Your account. When you sign in with LinkedIn, we receive your name, email address, profile picture, and your LinkedIn account identifier.
  • Your study activity. As you practice, we record the questions you attempt, the answers you choose, how long you take, and your score history.
  • A session cookie so you stay signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking.

How we use it

We use your data to run the service — signing you in, tracking your progress, and tailoring your practice. We also retain your study activity and use it to improve and train Chiron, and for our legitimate business purposes. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention and removing your identity

We retain your account and study history unless you ask us to remove it.

To request removal, email chiron-gdpr-removal-request@thrivetech.ai from the email address on your account. When we process your request we will:

  • Permanently delete your identifying account information — your name, email address, profile picture, and LinkedIn identifier — and replace it with an anonymous identifier that is not linked back to you.
  • Your account cannot be recovered after this. You would need to create a new one to use Chiron again.

Your past study activity is retained in this anonymized form — no longer tied to your identity — and may continue to be used to improve and train Chiron and for our business purposes.

Where your data lives

Chiron runs on Cloudflare. Your account and study records are stored in Cloudflare’s database and object storage. Access to the application is restricted while Chiron is in private beta.

Contact

Removal requests: chiron-gdpr-removal-request@thrivetech.ai
Other privacy questions: privacy@thrivetech.ai
